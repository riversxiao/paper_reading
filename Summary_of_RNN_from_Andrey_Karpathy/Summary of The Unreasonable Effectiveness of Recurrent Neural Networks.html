This is an article written by Andrey Karpathy explaining the RNN model. Here is a brief summary of the article. 
The first part is a step by step tutorial of a RNN model and its difference compared with previous NN model.
In previous models, information directly feed forward to hidden layer and to the output layer, while in RNN model, Information will circulate inside the hidden layers across time steps which will help remembering the order of the sequence.

Given the first character , for example “d", RNN will output the distribution function of the next character, then,we get a sample from the distribution, suppose it is an  “i” and we feed ‘d’ from previous hidden state and 'i’  for input layer into the network again we will get the next word ’s’ , this will continue until the terminal state .
